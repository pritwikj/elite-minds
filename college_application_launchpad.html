Program Overview

College Application Launch Pad is a comprehensive one-day workshop at UC Berkeley designed to jumpstart the college application process for high school students of all grade levels. In just 8 hours (9 AM - 5 PM), participants will receive expert guidance, hands-on application support, and insider strategies to strengthen their college admissions journey.

Who It's For

Unlike many programs that focus exclusively on rising seniors, this workshop welcomes students from all high school grades (9-12), providing grade-specific tracks that address the unique needs and timelines of each academic year.

Key Benefits

Expert UC Berkeley Mentorship

Learn from current UC Berkeley students and alumni who understand the admissions process firsthand.

Grade-Tailored Approach

Customized sessions based on your grade level—from freshman exploration to senior application completion.

Comprehensive Coverage

Tackle all aspects of college preparation in one intensive day.

Campus Experience

Actionable Takeaways

Leave with concrete next steps and materials for your college journey.

Program Structure

Morning Session (9 AM - 12 PM)

  • Welcome and introduction to college admissions landscape
  • Grade-specific breakout sessions
  • Application component workshops (Common App, personal statements)

Lunch Break (12 PM - 1 PM)

  • Networking lunch with mentors and peers
  • Mini-campus tour

Afternoon Session (1 PM - 5 PM)

  • Essay development workshops
  • Activity profile enhancement
  • School selection strategies
  • Financial aid overview
  • Individual consultation blocks
