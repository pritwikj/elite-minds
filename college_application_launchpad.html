Program Overview

The UC Application Launch Pad is an intensive, one-day online workshop, fully led by current Cal students and alumni. We'll guide you through every step you need to take to maximize your chances of getting into a top UC campus—sharing the real strategies, mindsets, and timelines that helped us succeed. You'll leave with a personalized roadmap and the confidence to tackle your UC applications head-on.

Who It's For

Unlike many programs that focus exclusively on rising seniors, this workshop welcomes students from all high school grades (9-12), providing grade-specific tracks that address the unique needs and timelines of each academic year.
