Week by Week Curriculum

Week 1: Introduction to AI and ML

  • Explore the fundamentals of AI/ML through real-world examples
  • Deep dive into Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Set up your AI development environment and explore the project lifecycle

Week 2: Python for AI Development

  • Master essential Python concepts for AI development
  • Learn to work with OpenAI and Anthropic APIs
  • Build your first AI-powered programs

Week 3: Working with Data and Prompts

  • Master the art of prompt engineering
  • Learn data collection and cleaning techniques
  • Design and test advanced prompt strategies

Week 4: ML and LLM Integration

  • Understand core machine learning concepts including supervised learning, classification, and evaluation metrics
  • Learn how LLMs work through concepts like tokenization and embeddings

Week 5: Project Discovery and Planning

  • Explore cutting-edge AI application opportunities
  • Define your project's scope and impact goals
  • Create a detailed project development roadmap

Week 6: Project Research

  • Learn best practices for LLM integration
  • Access curated AI resources and datasets
  • Begin building your project foundation

Week 7: Development

  • Tackle common AI/LLM challenges
  • Master debugging strategies
  • Create your project's first working version

Week 8: Testing & Feedback

  • Learn AI application testing methods
  • Present initial project version
  • Receive and incorporate peer feedback

Week 9: Final Development

  • Explore AI safety and ethics
  • Optimize project performance
  • Prepare for final presentation

Week 10: Launch Day

  • Present your AI project to industry experts
  • Receive AI Developer Certification
  • Plan your next steps in AI development
