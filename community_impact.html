Week by Week Curriculum

Week 1

  • Explore diverse social impact opportunities and develop your unique "passion thesis"
  • Analyze successful youth-led projects that gained university recognition

Week 2

  • Refine project ideas through structured feedback and research
  • Create initial project vision statement and impact goals

Week 3

  • Master ethical community research and stakeholder interview techniques
  • Map existing community initiatives in your area of interest

Week 4

  • Conduct structured community interviews and analyze findings
  • Create a compelling narrative around community need

Week 5

  • Transform research insights into actionable solutions using design thinking
  • Develop project prototypes with community input

Week 6

  • Master compelling grant proposal structure and impact statements
  • Begin drafting your grant proposal with project objectives

Week 7

  • Launch initial project components
  • Build your portfolio with real-time impact documentation

Week 8

  • Finalize grant proposal and create a compelling pitch presentation
  • Practice presentation skills with expert feedback

Week 9

  • Create visual representations of your impact
  • Develop compelling project narratives for college applications

Week 10

  • Present to a panel of T20 university researchers and alumni
  • Receive Community Change Leader Certification
