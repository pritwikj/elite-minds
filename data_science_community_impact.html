Week by Week Curriculum

Week 1: Introduction to Data Science & Social Impact

  • What is data science?
  • Overview of social impact research
  • Project planning and proposal basics

Week 2: Research Questions & Python Basics

  • Formulating effective research questions
  • Introduction to Python and Jupyter Notebooks
  • Basic programming concepts

Week 3: Data Collection & Cleaning

  • Finding and exploring datasets
  • Data cleaning with pandas
  • Understanding variables and data structure

Week 4: Data Visualization

  • Visualizing data with matplotlib and seaborn
  • Principles of effective data storytelling

Week 5: Statistics & Analysis

  • Descriptive statistics (mean, median, mode, correlation)
  • Introduction to basic statistical analysis in Python
  • Optional: Introduction to modeling and regression

Week 6: Presenting Research & Next Steps

  • Presenting research findings
  • Reflecting on impact and learning
  • Exploring further opportunities in data science

Possible Final Project Themes

  • Education Inequity (using Census or school district data)
  • Climate Impacts (air pollution, temperature change, flood zones)
  • Healthcare Access (insurance, hospital density, health outcomes)
  • Housing & Gentrification (rent prices, evictions, demographics)
  • Online Misinformation (social media trends, sentiment analysis)
  • Criminal Justice (incarceration rates, bail reform data)
  • Mental Health (youth mental health, access to care, stress factors)
