Taught By

Dr. George Mastorakos

Dr. George Mastorakos is a physician and AI architect at the forefront of medical technology. A graduate of the Mayo Clinic Alix School of Medicine, he leads the development of FDA-cleared AI tools for brain imaging at Cortechs.ai, enhancing diagnosis and patient care across hospitals. Previously at the Mayo Clinic, he led machine learning initiatives for COVID-19 triage and patient communication systems. With advanced training in biochemistry and biomimicry, Dr. Mastorakos brings a rare blend of clinical insight and technical expertise. His work bridges medicine and machine learning, and has been featured in top journals and academic conferences. Committed to building ethical, explainable AI, he is helping shape the future of human-centered healthcare.

Week by Week Curriculum

Week 1: Foundations of AI & Its Role in Medicine

  • Fundamentals of AI/ML in healthcare and how AI learns from medical data
  • Key medical applications: diagnostics, imaging, mental health
  • Case studies: AlphaFold, Med-PaLM 2, PENSIEVE-AI
  • Debate: AI's promise vs. hype in clinical practice

Week 2: AI in Brain & Body Imaging

  • AI for MRI & neuroimaging: glioma, dementia, seizures
  • Surgical assistance and real-time intraoperative AI
  • Wearables & digital biomarkers: Oura Ring, Teva's Parkinson's tools
  • Interactive MRI demo and future hospital AI brainstorm

Week 3: Personalized & Genomic Medicine with AI

  • AI in genomics, preventive care, and treatment planning
  • Quantum AI & nanotech in early detection
  • Cancer treatment guided by genetic profiles; bioelectronic therapies
  • Case study and ethics discussion: Who owns your DNA data?

Week 4: Biomimicry, Quantum Thinking & Bioelectricity

  • Nature-inspired AI: biomimetic designs in medicine
  • Quantum principles and bioelectric signaling in healthcare
  • Sharklet, Velcro, and bioelectric medicine case studies
  • Workshop: Invent a biomimetic or quantum-inspired AI tool

Week 5: Responsible AI – Ethics, Privacy, and Empathy

  • Data privacy, equity, bias, and explainability in clinical AI
  • Empathy in mental health AI and algorithmic bias in diagnosis
  • Debate: Can AI ever be truly empathetic?
  • Design: Build an ethically mindful AI tool

Week 6: Capstone Projects & Vision for the Future

  • Final presentations: Showcase AI-based medical solutions
  • Peer review, feedback, and group reflection
  • Propose an AI solution integrating course themes
  • Discuss future innovations and your role in shaping healthcare
