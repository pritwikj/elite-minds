Week by Week Curriculum

Week 1: Discover — What Makes a Great Digital Product?

  • What is a product? What is a tech business?
  • Role of product manager & strategist
  • Product vs. project vs. business
  • Program overview + Final project intro

Week 2: Define the Problem & Audience

  • User personas
  • Problem definition & customer interviews
  • Basics of market sizing and validation

Week 3: MVP & Agile Thinking

  • Agile vs. waterfall
  • MVP vs. full product
  • Building lean roadmaps

Week 4: Financial Modeling for Tech Products

  • Pricing models (freemium, SaaS, one-time)
  • Unit economics
  • Break-even analysis

Week 5: Prototyping with No-Code Tools

  • Intro to Figma, Glide, or Canva (based on team skills)
  • How to turn an idea into a digital mockup or working prototype

Week 6: Storytelling with Strategy

  • Product strategy 101 (vision, roadmap, metrics)
  • Competitive analysis
  • Pitch structure: problem > solution > business > ask

Week 7: Final Touches – Rehearsals & Feedback

  • Pitch deck reviews
  • Financial and prototype reviews
  • Peer feedback sessions
  • Rehearse elevator pitch

Week 8: Final Showcase – Techpreneur Demo Day

  • Final Deliverables:
  • Pitch deck
  • MVP or clickable prototype
  • Strategy & financial one-pager
