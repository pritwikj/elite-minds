Week by Week Curriculum

Week 1: Introduction to Cybersecurity

  • Learn what cybersecurity is and why it matters
  • Explore exciting career paths in the cybersecurity field
  • Set up your beginner-friendly learning environment

Week 2: Internet & Computer Basics

  • Understand how the internet works
  • Learn essential computer and networking concepts
  • Discover how information travels online

Week 3: Digital Safety Fundamentals

  • Create strong passwords and understand authentication
  • Learn about common online threats
  • Practice basic security measures everyone should know

Week 4: Introduction to Ethical Hacking

  • Understand what ethical hacking means
  • Learn about white hat vs. black hat hackers
  • Try your first simple security tools

Week 5: Web Security Basics

  • Learn how websites work
  • Discover common website vulnerabilities
  • Practice identifying basic security issues

Week 6: Project Discovery

  • Explore beginner-friendly cybersecurity project ideas
  • Choose a project that matches your interests
  • Create a simple plan for your project

Week 7: Project Building - Part 1

  • Start working on your project with guided support
  • Learn problem-solving techniques
  • Build the foundation of your security project

Week 8: Project Building - Part 2

  • Continue developing your project
  • Learn how to test your work
  • Troubleshoot common issues with expert help

Week 9: Finalizing Your Project

  • Complete and polish your cybersecurity project
  • Learn how to document your work professionally
  • Prepare for your presentation

Week 10: Presentation & Next Steps

  • Present your project to the class and experts
  • Receive your Cybersecurity Foundations Certification
  • Explore how to continue your cybersecurity journey
