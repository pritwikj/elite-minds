Week by Week Curriculum

Week 1

  • Introduction to biomedical engineering fundamentals
  • Explore real-world medical device applications
  • Learn key principles that drive medical innovation

Week 2

  • Master TinkerCAD basics for digital design
  • Create first 3D medical device models
  • Understand core principles of CAD modeling for healthcare

Week 3

  • Learn vital signs and medical data analysis
  • Practice with spreadsheet software for health tracking
  • Transform raw health data into meaningful insights

Week 4

  • Explore medical imaging fundamentals
  • Master Pixlr for image analysis
  • Apply digital tools to analyze medical imagery

Week 5

  • Study the engineering design process
  • Choose specialized project track
  • Define your unique medical innovation focus

Week 6

  • Conduct project research
  • Develop initial project concepts
  • Transform research insights into actionable solutions

Week 7

  • Advance project development
  • Tackle design challenges
  • Iterate and refine your medical innovation

Week 8

  • Present initial designs
  • Incorporate peer feedback
  • Optimize your solution through collaboration

Week 9

  • Finalize project development
  • Prepare presentation materials
  • Polish your project for professional showcase

Week 10

  • Final project presentations
  • Receive Biomedical Engineering certification
  • Celebrate your journey as a medical innovator
